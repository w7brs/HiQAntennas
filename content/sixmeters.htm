Using Hi-Q Antennas on 6 meters

To be able to efficiently utilize the 6 meter band on any Hi-Q Antenna, one MUST utilize an antenna analyzer, find the 5/8, 3/4 or even the MULTIPLE wavelength resonant point. This is due to the fact that the total antenna height exceeds 1/4 wavelength. Some hams have found it advantageous to dedicated a small 6 meter antenna, in addition to their Hi-Q Antenna.
