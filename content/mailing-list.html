Hi-Q-Antennas Mailing List

The Hi-Q-Antennas e-mail mailing list is for current and future users of Hi-Q-Antennas products, to discuss installation and operation of the products, etc. To keep the spammers away, you must first subscribe to the list, before you can post a message.

Subscribe to the Mailing List
You can subscribe to the Hi-Q-Antennas Mailing List by filling out the following form. You will be sent email requesting confirmation, to prevent others from gratuitously subscribing you. This is a hidden list, which means that the list of members is available only to the list administrator.

Post a Message to the Mailing List
To post a message to the Hi-Q-Antennas Mailing List, you must first subscribe to the list (see above). After you have successfully subscribed, you may post a message to the list by sending an e-mail to list@hiqantennas.com.
