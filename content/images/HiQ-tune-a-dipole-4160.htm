Hi-Q-Antennas has gone back 8 years and reintroduced the Tune-A-Dipole (TAD) HF Portable antenna. The Tune-A-Dipole is available in both a Manually Tunable version and a Remotely Tunable (Motorized) version.

These TAD's (Tune-A-Dipoles) are the STATE of the ART Hi-Q design. Developed for our military, where the criteria for portability, fast OUT-of Box set up and frequency coverage. It is within the HAM and Commercial use range.

Ideal for DX Expeditions! Great Performer and portable!

For portable operation, the TAD antenna can be stored, transported shipped in the OPTIONAL SKB Rugged case with handles and wheels. Size; 50"X18"X7". This case is practical for the Hi-Q-4 and the Hi-Q-5 series TAD/NVIS antennas.

Technical details:

  • Shipping weight:
        Mil version: 40 lbs
    Ham/Commercial version 30 lbs.
  • Continuously Tunable from 1.8 to 30 MHz, it is Rotatable
  • Figure 8 lobe with appx. 2.6 dB gain
  • 12 or optional 24 VDC motors appx. 1 amp draw.
  • 4" dia. Hi-Q Coil with an unloaded "Q" of greater than 400!
  • Ham/Commercial versions using #12 aerospace wire, Mil version .100"quot; dia.
  • Turning Radius:
        15’ with 7’ whip and 32" CapHat
        8’ with 2’ ext. rod and 48” CapHat and NO WHIP!!!
